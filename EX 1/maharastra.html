Maharashtra

Demographic Profile:

Population: 112.37 million (approx.)
Capital: Mumbai
Official Language: Marathi
Major Cities: Mumbai, Pune, Nagpur, Nashik, Aurangabad

Maharashtra is one of the most populous and economically significant states in India, known for its diverse culture and industrial prowess.

Economic Profile:

GSDP: US$339.85 billion in 2019-2020 (approx.)
Major Industries: Finance, Entertainment (Bollywood), Manufacturing (Automobiles, Pharmaceuticals, Textiles), IT, Agriculture.
Agricultural Products: Sugarcane, Cotton, Grapes, Mangoes, Onions.

Maharashtra has a highly diversified economy, with Mumbai serving as the financial capital of India.

Infrastructure Development:

The state government is focused on improving infrastructure, including:
  • Extensive road and highway network.
  • Mumbai Metro and other urban transport projects.
  • Development of ports and airports.
  • Industrial and IT parks.
  • Focus on renewable energy and sustainable development.
