Rajasthan

Demographic Profile:

Population: 68.5 million (2011 census)
Capital: Jaipur
Official Language: Hindi, Rajasthani
Major Cities: Jaipur, Jodhpur, Udaipur, Ajmer, Kota, Bikaner, Jaisalmer

Rajasthan is the largest state of India by area and is known for its rich cultural heritage and historical significance.

Infrastructure Development:

The state government is focusing on improving infrastructure, including:
  • Road and highway development.
  • Development of renewable energy projects (solar and wind).
  • Expansion of airport facilities (Jaipur, Jodhpur, Udaipur).
  • Development of industrial areas and special economic zones.
  • Improved water management in arid regions.
